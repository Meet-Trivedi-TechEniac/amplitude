About SpeedZone

Born from a passion for speed, built on a foundation of precision engineering and automotive excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, SpeedZone started as a small garage operation with a big dream: to make high-performance automotive experiences accessible to everyone. What began with two mechanics and a shared passion has grown into a full-service performance hub.

Today, we serve over 500 clients annually, from weekend enthusiasts to professional racers. Our state-of-the-art facility houses the latest diagnostic equipment, a full dyno room, and a team of ASE-certified technicians.
